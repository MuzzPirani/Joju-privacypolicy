Apple Platform Services & Crash Reporting As an app distributed through the Apple App Store, Joju is subject to Apple's standard iOS platform services. There are two important things to be aware of: Crash Reports (Optional — Device-Level Setting) Apple's iOS includes an optional system-level feature that can send anonymous crash reports to app developers when an app crashes. Whether this data is shared is controlled entirely by you (or the device owner) through your iPhone or iPad settings under Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements. If this setting is enabled on your device, Apple may share anonymized diagnostic crash data with us solely for the purpose of identifying and fixing technical issues. This data does not contain any personal information and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. If this setting is disabled, no crash data is shared with us.
