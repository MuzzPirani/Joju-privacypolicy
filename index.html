01

Introduction

Joju ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy — and the privacy of children who use the App. This Privacy Policy explains how Joju handles information in connection with your use of the App, available exclusively on the Apple App Store for iPhone and iPad.

Please read this policy carefully. By downloading or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

02

The Short Version

We collect nothing. We store nothing. We transmit nothing.

Joju is a children's application that operates entirely on your device using on-device AI models. All processing — including all AI-powered features — happens locally on your iPhone or iPad. No data of any kind is ever sent to us, to any server, or to any third party.

03

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data — from children or adults.

Joju does not collect, access, store, transmit, share, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, Apple ID, device ID, etc.)
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Usage data or behavioral analytics
  • Diagnostics or crash reports sent to us
  • User-generated content
  • Photos, camera, or microphone input (unless explicitly stated below)
  • Contacts, calendars, or any other device data
  • Health, financial, or biometric information
  • Browsing or search history
  • Identifiers for advertising (IDFA or similar)
04

How the App Works — On-Device AI Processing Only

All functionality of Joju, including all artificial intelligence and machine learning features, is performed entirely on your device using Apple's on-device frameworks (such as Core ML). This means:

  • All AI models run locally on your device — no input or output is sent to any external server or AI provider.
  • No internet connection is required for any core App functionality.
  • No data is uploaded to any server, cloud service, or remote endpoint at any time.
  • No account creation is required or possible.
  • No data leaves your device as a result of using this App.

Any content generated or processed by the App remains exclusively on your device and is fully under your control.

05

Apple Platform Services & Crash Reporting

As an app distributed through the Apple App Store, Joju is subject to Apple's standard iOS platform services. There are two important things to be aware of:

Crash Reports (Optional — Device-Level Setting)

Apple's iOS includes an optional system-level feature that can send anonymous crash reports to app developers when an app crashes. Whether this data is shared is controlled entirely by you (or the device owner) through your iPhone or iPad settings under Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements. If this setting is enabled on your device, Apple may share anonymized diagnostic crash data with us solely for the purpose of identifying and fixing technical issues. This data does not contain any personal information and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. If this setting is disabled, no crash data is shared with us.

App Store & In-App Purchases
Apple independently collects information related to App Store downloads and any in-app purchases in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or have access to payment or personal information associated with these transactions.

We do not have control over or access to any other data Apple may collect independently through iOS system functions.

06

Third-Party Services

Joju does not integrate any third-party services that collect or transmit user data. Specifically, the App contains no:

  • Third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude)
  • Advertising networks or SDKs (e.g., AdMob, Meta Audience Network)
  • Crash reporting tools (e.g., Crashlytics, Sentry)
  • Cloud-based AI or machine learning APIs (e.g., OpenAI, Google Gemini)
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media SDKs or login features
  • Any other third-party SDK that accesses or transmits user data

In-App Purchases

If the App offers in-app purchases, these transactions are processed exclusively through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or process any payment information. All purchase records are handled solely by Apple in accordance with their terms and privacy policy.

Data Security

Because we do not collect, store, or transmit any user data, there is no user data on our end to secure. All content generated within the App is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, and iOS's application sandboxing architecture.

Joju is available worldwide. Because we collect no personal data from any user in any country, the data transfer, localization, and consent requirements of international privacy frameworks — including GDPR (EU/EEA), UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA (California), PIPEDA (Canada), LGPD (Brazil), PDPA (Thailand/Singapore), POPIA (South Africa), and others — are satisfied by the App's zero-collection architecture.
